# Mentors

*Who the wisdom on this wiki comes from. One page per mentor, with bio and the principles each contributed.*

---

The wisdom on this wiki is sourced from conversations with operators who have built businesses worth the bar. Not from books, not from podcasts, not from generic advice columns. Specific people who have lived the answers to specific questions and were generous enough to walk through them in a real conversation.

Each mentor page documents who the person is, what they have built that earns them a voice on this wiki, and which principles on this wiki trace back to them.

## How mentors get added

A mentor lands on this wiki when three things are true. They have built or operated a business at a level that produces wisdom most operators do not have access to. They have had a substantive conversation with the wiki's stewards on principles relevant to building an agentic business. They have consented to being named as the source of principles attributed to them.

The wiki is selective on purpose. Adding a mentor is a credibility statement.
