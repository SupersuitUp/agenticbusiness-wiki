# Mentors

*Who the wisdom on this wiki comes from. One page per mentor, with bio and the principles each contributed.*

---

The wisdom on this wiki is sourced from specific people who have lived the answers to specific questions. The primary form is a real conversation with the wiki's stewards. In rare cases, the source is a book that does the same work a conversation would, written by someone with the operator credentials to back it. No podcasts, no generic advice columns.

Each mentor page documents who the person is, what they have built that earns them a voice on this wiki, and which principles on this wiki trace back to them.

## How mentors get added

A mentor lands on this wiki when three things are true. They have built or operated a business at a level that produces wisdom most operators do not have access to. They have either had a substantive conversation with the wiki's stewards on principles relevant to building an agentic business, or have written a book on the subject that meets the same substantive bar. They have consented to being named as the source of principles attributed to them (or, in the book case, their published work is the consent).

The wiki is selective on purpose. Adding a mentor is a credibility statement.
