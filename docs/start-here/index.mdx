## What an agentic business is

A business is **agentic** when it applies AI to its highest and best use: multiplying the human potential of the people inside it, in service of a mission that existed before the AI did. The goal is a business that delivers value at a scale that was previously impossible while keeping the irreplaceably human elements intact. Headcount reduction is the wrong target.

Some of the principles on this wiki are AI-aware. Others are timeless business principles that good operators have always known. Both belong here, because an agentic business is still a business, and the principles that make any business durable are the substrate.

## Who this wiki is for

The reader is the principal of an agentic business: the founder, the CEO, the controlling operator. The one whose name is on the building, whose capital is at risk, and whose decisions set the direction nobody else is allowed to set. If the business stops compounding when you stop showing up, you are who this wiki is written to.

The principal has decided to apply AI seriously. They want to do it without losing the soul of what they built. They are hungry for advice from people who have already navigated the harder parts of building. This wiki exists so they walk into the next decision with the vocabulary, the principles, and the cautionary tales already absorbed.

This wiki is not for AI hobbyists, not for vendors selling into principals, and not for implementers looking for craft guidance. The implementer-facing material lives at [appliedai.wiki](https://appliedai.wiki). This wiki speaks to the principal whose call it ultimately is.

## Attribution

Every principle on this wiki names the mentor whose conversation surfaced it. When a quote is verbatim, it is in blockquotes with the mentor's name and the date of the conversation. The wiki is the operators' wisdom captured, not the wiki author's invention. Where a principle has been articulated by multiple mentors, both are credited.

## North star

The operators behind this wiki want to make it easier for a principal to build a business that lasts: profitable, durable, AI-properly-applied, and worth a life. The wiki is the place to put what we learn from the people who have already done this well, so that the next principal does not have to discover it from scratch.
